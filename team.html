The Roots of Our Team

Norwood Landscape slowly became a family business almost twenty years ago when Jerry’s son Mark joined the company, and with the help of some really close friends remains that way today. Combined, our team has over 60 years of landscaping experience…all with Norwood Landscape. Having worked together for so long is one of our strongest assets. This continuity assures us that as we continue to grow, our work remains top notch. And of course, throughout the year we get through the busy seasons with the help of our invaluable part-time staff, many of which are friends of the family!
