A Message from Mark

During one of my wife's recent quests to find a trendy DIY project for us to do together, she came across the use of pallets in the yard and landscape. After looking through many photos on the internet we settled on using a pallet as a vertical planter. Later that afternoon we headed out to the shop and looked through the many stacks of pallets for the "perfect pallet"!

The following morning we headed to a local nursery and picked out just the right type of flowers, ones that would grow well in the tight spaces. We started out using coco liner to fill the spaces for planting but it was a bit bulky and didn't allow much room for plant growth and roots.

Luckily, Kaitlynn had some leftover burlap sitting around from our wedding reception which proved to be a much better choice. When all was said and done we had a beautiful vertical pallet planter that filled one area of our long wood fence. It really brightened up a dull area which was in desperate need of some color!

The best part of this project, aside from getting my wife to work in the garden with me, is that we gave an old pallet new life! What a great way to repurpose and recycle.

Check out the photos to see what we designed and watch for updated pictures on our Facebook page and future newsletters.

Your feedback is always welcome!

Regards,
Mark
