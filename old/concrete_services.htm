OUR CONCRETE SOLUTIONS

Norwood Landscape has recently expanded its services and now offers a cost effective alternative to ripping out and replacing your existing concrete. As an authorized CTi dealer, Norwood Landscape can now enhance the look of your concrete and add value to your property by resurfacing your existing slab. Choose from a large selection of colors and patterns that will make your home or office unique and stand out. Our indoor applications will give your basement or garage floor that “custom built” look. Add your company logo to the concrete and give your business a rich, professional look, guaranteed to impress your customers. Our industrial applications are perfect for garage bays, warehouses, and other high traffic areas.

Benefits of applying the CTI system
- Backed by up to a 10 year warranty
- No damage to existing landscape
- Increases the strength and durability
- Stain resistant
- Easy to maintain
- Saves time and money

- Walkways

- Warehouses and many more
